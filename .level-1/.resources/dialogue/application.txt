As you search for the treasure, you will use `cd` to enter new locations, `ls` to explore them, and `cat` to interact with the objects in the environment. Certain special objects won't work with `cat`. These items will always end withe extension `.sh`, and to interact with these items, just type `./` followed by their name. Let's say we find a clue named `clue.sh` and we want to look at it. To interact with it, we type `./clue.sh`. Let's try to use some of the skills we've learned!
